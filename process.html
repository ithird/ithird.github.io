Our Process

We help complete your needs in 4 steps:

  • Submit your enquiry

    Tell us your need and our staff will contact you in 24 hours to understand your requirement in detail.

  • Provide a Quote

    Our staff will revert to you with a quote as quickly as possible from a verified list of suppliers. We also help with a sample demo whenever needed.

  • Quote to Production

    Once the quote is accepted and an order is placed with the supplier , we track the order to completion until it is onboarded for transportation. Along the way , we help ensure any of your requirements around labeling , destination regulatory requirements are addressed by the supplier and inspection of the product is done before loading for shipment.

  • Destination delivery

    We help track your order until it is delivered to destination.
