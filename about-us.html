Why Choose Us?

Our Services

From new sellers to mature businesses, we provide tailored services to support you.

  • Professional Sourcing

    We help you connect with verified and fully qualified suppliers in China, Vietnam , Korea, Taiwan and Japan.

  • Local Point of Contact

    We act as your local point of contact, provide your requirements to suppliers and follow up orders until they reach their destinations.

  • Private Label

    We help liaise with suppliers to enable customised logos , packaging for your products to differentiate them from competitors.

  • Quality and Compliance

    We ensure the products are quality checked and comply with regulatory requirements of respective import markets. The export qualifications of the suppliers are made very transparent. We also help liaise with quality testing/inspection partners.

  • Shipping/Air Freight

    We offer services to ensure products are loaded for Air or Shipping freights as per your needs.
